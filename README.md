# ResolveNow: Your Platform for Online Complaints


The Online Complaint Registration and Management System is a web-based software application designed to allow individuals or organizations to submit, manage, and track complaints efficiently. The system helps optimize the complaint-handling process and empowers organizations to build an effective safety and service management system.

By providing a centralized platform, this system streamlines complaint resolution, ensures compliance with industry standards and regulatory guidelines, and improves overall customer satisfaction.
